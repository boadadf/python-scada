This is the Model Train Simulation module.
1. Press the "RaspberryPi" button to connect or disconnect from the IO driver.
2. Use the "Left Control" to change the switch state (Straight or Turn).
3. If the switches are not in the same state (both Straight or both Turn), an alarm will be triggered.
4. A popup will appear, prompting you to set both switches to the same state.
5. Navigate to the Alarm tab to acknowledge the alarm and resolve the issue.
