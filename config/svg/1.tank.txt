This is the Tank Simulation module.
1. Go to the Communications tab and connect to the WaterTank server.
2. Click the "PUMP" button to start pumping water.
3. Open the "VALVE IN" to fill the tank with water.
4. Click the "VALVE OUT" to empty the tank.
5. Observe the alarm when the water level drops below 10.
6. Navigate to the Alarm tab to acknowledge the alarm.
